Lost In An Unknown Island.

It Is Your Job To Survive.

Gather. Hunt. Fish.

Farm. Breed. Maintain.

Craft. Build. Research.

Find your own sources of food.

To survive the day after today.

Search through various sources of life.

That is eatable or that is not.

Trap, track, and hunt.

To secure a meal for the night.

Sit by the riverbed.

Waiting for a will be guest to stop by.

Meat, fish, plant, or none at all.

What will be your dinner tonight.

The stay may not be short.

Escape may not be easy.

Grow your own crops and material.

Supply may deplete.

Remember to breed when possible.

Population is not always high.

The islands are very small.

The ecosystem is very frail.

Or disregard all of this.

And await a fate worse than now.

With nothing to start with.

You have to create your own.

Crafting is not simple.

And tools will not hold.

Shelter is not everywhere.

The weather will often change.

Improvise and modify.

May be what you need to survive.

The things that we need.

And the things that we have.
