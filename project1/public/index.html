The worst trip ever!


Have you ever wanted to be stranded on a remote island in the middle of nowhere so that you don't have to worry about work, school, family, and everything else? Now you can with this trip on the S.S.Mori islands a unknown island in a unknown location! Where your will survive, explore, and escape the island or fail to do so and die. You are on your own!
